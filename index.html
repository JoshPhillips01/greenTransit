Who Are We?

Green Transit is an organisation striving to take action against climate change and greenhouse gases specifically with vehicle emissions. We specialise in informing and offering solutions to the current global problem at hand with climate change. Although climate change seems to be such a big problem, a small action can start a big difference. Here at Green Transit, cutting down on vehicle emissions is our primary concern as it contributes to 28% of greenhouse gases. Everyday passenger cars contribute the most to this.

Our main course of action is to change the way the everyday household family travels to and from work or school, whether it be via train or bus.

Every little bit makes a difference in saving the place we call home and making it a healthier place for our future generations.
